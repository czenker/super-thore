About Super Thore

This is a game about a super hero called Thore. The story is loosly based on the adventures of an italian plumber and his brother in the 80s, but with a new protagonist and in a modern setting.

How is this game played?

You can use your keyboard or the buttons on screen.

Your goal is to reach the end of the level. You can collect Power Ups from the boxes with a question mark and become Stärker! Just remember: You only have One Heart – if you fail, you have to start from the beginning. Once you are finished you advance to the Next Level.

No there are no eastereggs. There is no reason to collect a mushroom, a flower, duck or finish the level at all.
